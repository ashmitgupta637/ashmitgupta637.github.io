About

Two disciplines, one build habit

I'm an Electronics & Communication Engineering undergrad at NSUT, currently interning as an Electronics Engineering Intern at BHEL, Noida, where I analyze industrial circuit schematics and evaluate PLC/SCADA workflows for heavy electrical systems.

On the software side, I build backend systems in Flask and SQLAlchemy — REST APIs with JWT authentication, role-based access, and clean, tested endpoints. On the hardware side, I've built things like an 8-bit DAC from scratch using an R‑2R ladder network.

I also compete: 250+ DSA problems solved across LeetCode, GeeksforGeeks, and AtCoder, and I lead squad drills as an NCC cadet.
